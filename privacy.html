Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2024. This policy describes how Urja CBG Consultants collects, uses, and protects the personal and technical data of our stakeholders in the renewable energy sector.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

1. Introduction 2. Information Collection 3. Use of Data 4. Cookies Policy 5. Data Security 6. Third-Party Sharing 7. Your Rights 8. Contact Details

1. Introduction

Welcome to Urja CBG Consultants. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. As an industrial consultancy firm specializing in Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG) solutions, we understand the importance of technical confidentiality and data integrity.

This privacy policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile applications, and any related services, sales, marketing, or events. When you visit our website and use our services, you trust us with your personal information.

2. Information Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services. The personal information we collect can include the following:

  • Professional Contact Data: Name, professional email address, phone number, and physical office address.
  • Project Specifics: Information regarding agricultural waste profiles, industrial site locations, and technical requirements provided during consultations.
  • Company Information: Business registration details, industry sector, and operational scale.

We also automatically collect certain information—such as your IP address and/or browser and device characteristics—when you visit our services. This information does not reveal your specific identity but may include device and usage information.

3. Use of Data

We use the information we collect or receive for various business purposes, including:

  • To facilitate project feasibility studies and technical proposals.
  • To send administrative information to you, such as updates on project timelines or changes to our terms.
  • To protect our services against technical vulnerabilities or industrial espionage.
  • To comply with legal obligations regarding environmental reporting and industrial certifications.

4. Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information. Cookies help us understand how users navigate our engineering portal and which technical resources are most utilized.

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function, particularly for secure client login areas.

Performance Cookies

Help us analyze site traffic to improve our technical resource delivery.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. Our infrastructure is protected by industrial-grade firewalls and end-to-end encryption for all technical documents.

However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our services is at your own risk.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, or to fulfill business obligations. This may include sharing technical data with:

  • Government bodies for regulatory environmental clearances.
  • Authorized engineering subcontractors involved in your specific CBG project.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers for secure data storage.

7. Your Rights

In some regions, such as the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights that allow you greater access to and control over your personal information. You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time.

If you are a resident in the EEA and you believe we are unlawfully processing your personal information, you also have the right to complain to your local data protection supervisory authority.
